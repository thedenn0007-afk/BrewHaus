FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book a table in advance? ›
Walk-ins are always welcome, but we recommend booking on weekends and public holidays as tables fill up fast. Reservations can be made through this form or by calling us directly.
Do you have vegan or gluten-free options? ›
Yes! We have a dedicated veg and vegan-friendly section on the menu. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Is BrewHaus available for corporate or event catering? ›
Absolutely. We offer catering packages for corporate lunches, birthday parties, and private events. Contact us via email at events@brewhaus.demo or use the form above for details and pricing.
Can I use multiple discount codes at once? ›
Generally, only one discount code can be applied per bill. However, the Student Discount and Happy Hour offer can be stacked for eligible customers between 3–5 PM on weekdays.
Do you offer franchise opportunities? ›
Yes! We're actively looking to expand across India. Send your details and city of interest to franchise@brewhaus.demo and our team will get back to you within 5 business days.
Is there free Wi-Fi at all outlets? ›
Yes, all four BrewHaus outlets offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi. The password is available on your receipt or you can ask our staff. No time limit — work as long as you like!
